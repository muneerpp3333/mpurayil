## FAQ

### What is SaaS Onboarding Flows and why does it matter?

SaaS Onboarding Flows is a critical architectural pattern for modern software systems. It matters because it directly impacts scalability, maintainability, and team velocity in production environments.

### How does React compare for SaaS Onboarding Flows?

React offers specific advantages for SaaS Onboarding Flows including strong typing, ecosystem support, and production-grade tooling. The choice depends on your team's expertise and project requirements.

### What are common mistakes with SaaS Onboarding Flows?

Common mistakes include premature optimization, insufficient observability, ignoring failure modes, and over-engineering the initial implementation. Start simple and iterate based on production data.
