## FAQ

### What is CQRS & Event Sourcing and why does it matter?

CQRS & Event Sourcing is a critical architectural pattern for modern software systems. It matters because it directly impacts scalability, maintainability, and team velocity in production environments.

### How does Spring Boot compare for CQRS & Event Sourcing?

Spring Boot offers specific advantages for CQRS & Event Sourcing including strong typing, ecosystem support, and production-grade tooling. The choice depends on your team's expertise and project requirements.

### What are common mistakes with CQRS & Event Sourcing?

Common mistakes include premature optimization, insufficient observability, ignoring failure modes, and over-engineering the initial implementation. Start simple and iterate based on production data.
